Digital Design

Here's a collection of my personal renders that I have made on blender. All materials and objects in the renders are produced by me.

Donut Render

This is my first render on Blender that I used to get used to the software. I learned how to create meshes and materials, how to alter colors and form, and how to set up lighting and the active camera.

Sublime Sky Room Render

In this render I experimented more with creating realistic forms. I also explored creating my own textures and background, and worked with the sun to create light instead of a lightbulb. My favorite part of the render is the realistic neon light on the wall, which really glows from the inside!

Puddle Street Rain Render

This is my first attempt at realistic rain and puddle rendering on a procedural street texture that I created myself.
