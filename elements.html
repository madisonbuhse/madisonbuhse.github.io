Architecture

Here's a collection of my best undergrad architecture projects.

First Year Winter Quarter '20

During my first year at Cal Poly I focused on linewights and creating coherent forms. I found a love for abstract imaging and blocking, which you can see in the first three images. Shown in the last picture is my own original design for a set of two story row homes.

First Year Spring Quarter '21

Over the duration of this quarter I experimented more with color and the forms of existing objects. We began the quarter with a found object abstraction which is shown in the two first images. We then moved onto creating a movie gallery within a parking garage, which is shown in the latter two images.

Second Year Winter Quarter '21

This quarter I focused on analyzing more objects/buildings and then made further abstractions and collages.

Second Year Spring Quarter '22

This quarter I was designing an apartment complex in downtown Los Angeles for low-income tenants. I worked with an already existing site which forced me to work within the bounds of reality. I was forced to constantly problem solve issues between my design and its real life context.
