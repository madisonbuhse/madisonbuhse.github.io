About Me

Hey there! Thank you for visiting my web portfolio. My name is Madison Buhse and I am digital designer. I studied Architecture for three years at California Polytechnic University in San Luis Obispo, California. My time spent there was worthwhile in many different aspects. I got to work with many professionals and other students and through that I developed a keen eye for balancing lineweights, scale, and space around and within objects. As part of the major I was required to make many posters, digital graphics, and renderings. Through doing that I developed a love for digital design that has now taken hold of me. I found that I enjoy being creative and creating original pieces of work, whether its a poster or a digital scene.

I have a very strong and rigorous work ethic and aim to make each project my best project. I love to collaborate with others in order to reach the intended message of the work at hand. I am versatile in the programs that I use and can pick up new programs very quickly. As of now I am proficient in all of the Adobe Creative Suite programs and also use Rhino and Blender.
